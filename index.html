Located in the heart of Aldeburgh, Becky's Coffee Space is specifically designed for those seeking a peaceful spot with internet access, delicious coffee, and snacks.

Whether you are a remote worker, student, local or traveller you are welcome here at Becky's Coffee Space.

Work and Study

We provide an ideal environment for those looking to work or study. We have fibre broadband, easily accessible usb and plug sockets and tables suited to both invividuals and groups.

Eat, Drink, Relax

Our quiet and relaxed environment is well suited to anyone looking to take some time out. Take a break to start that book you've been meaning to read or enjoy one of our selection of board game with a friend.
